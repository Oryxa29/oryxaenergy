3-6kW Single Phase High Voltage IP65

The ORYX-HH3K~6K single-phase inverter delivers reliable performance with a 5-year warranty and premium components from Infineon and ON. 3kW~8kW Hybrid Inverter

3-8kW Single Phase Low Voltage IP65

The ORYX-HL3K~8K is a versatile residential energy solution with dual MPPT trackers, a wide input voltage range (45V–58V), and peak efficiency of 99.9%. Built-in AC/DC breakers and rapid current monitoring provide safe, simplified installation. Seamless on/off-grid switching is supported, and the user-friendly app offers real-time remote monitoring and control, making it ideal for households seeking both efficiency and convenience.

8-12kW Hybrid Inverter

8-12kW Three Phase High Voltage IP65

The ORYX-HH8K~12K three-phase high voltage inverter combines efficiency, reliability, and flexibility. It supports a wide battery range (125V–800V) and uses DSP control to keep harmonic distortion below 3%, ensuring grid stability. With WiFi and web access, users benefit from seamless remote monitoring. It adapts to multiple operating modes, including 100% single-phase unbalanced load off-grid. The IP65 fanless design ensures durability, while less than 10ms switch time guarantees uninterrupted power.

10-16kW Hybrid Inverter

10-16kW Single Phase Low Voltage IP65

The ORYX-HL10~16K is a robust single-phase low-voltage hybrid inverter for homes and microgrids, supporting 10–16kW systems. It delivers up to 275A battery current for large lithium packs and peak loads, with 3 MPPTs and 150% PV oversizing for maximum flexibility. 8-24kW Hybrid Inverter

8k-24kW Three Phase Low Voltage IP66

The ORYX-HL8~24K is a high-performance three-phase low-voltage hybrid inverter designed for residential and small commercial use, supporting 8–24kW systems.
