Water Heaters

Hybrid PV Water Heater

60L-200L Water Heater

The Hybrid PV Water Heater integrates solar photovoltaic technology with grid power to ensure a continuous and efficient hot water supply. It automatically prioritizes solar heating when sunlight is available and switches to grid power when needed, guaranteeing reliable performance under all conditions. With advanced MPPT control, strong safety protection, and simple installation, it offers an ideal solution for households, businesses, and industrial users seeking both energy savings and stable hot water.

PV Direct Water Heater

80L-360L Water Heater

The PV Direct Water Heater is a cost-effective system that connects solar panels directly to the heating element, providing hot water without the need for complicated pipelines or circulation systems. It is easy to install, requires minimal maintenance, and ensures reliable operation with built-in protection functions. Designed for residential and small commercial applications, it reduces dependence on electricity while delivering consistent and efficient hot water.

Solar Thermal Water Heater

120L-300L Water Heater

The Solar Thermal Water Heater uses high-efficiency collectors to convert solar radiation into heat, delivering abundant hot water while significantly reducing energy costs. With various installation options, it adapts to different climates and building types, from residential homes to large institutions. The system features reliable insulation, strong durability, and safety protections, making it a trusted choice for sustainable water heating solutions across multiple applications.

All-in-One Solar Water Heater

60L-360L Water Heater

The All-in-One Solar Water Heater combines the collector, storage tank, and intelligent control into a single compact unit. This integrated design simplifies installation and saves space while ensuring high efficiency and consistent hot water output. Equipped with safety protections and smart temperature control, it is ideal for residential and commercial use where convenience, reliability, and energy savings are equally important.
