29.9kW~60kW Hybrid Inverter

29.9-60kW Three Phase High Voltage IP65

The ORYX-HH29.9~60K series is a high-capacity three-phase hybrid inverter range, designed for commercial and industrial applications from 29.9kW to 60kW. It supports both on-grid and off-grid operation with 4 MPPTs and advanced energy management for efficient power conversion. Equipped with SiC technology, it ensures superior efficiency, stability, and performance in harsh conditions. Dual battery ports allow independent 100A charge/discharge or 200A parallel operation, delivering flexible energy storage options. This robust design provides a comprehensive, scalable, and highly competitive solution for large-scale energy projects.

125kW Hybrid Inverter

125kW Three Phase High Voltage IP65

The ORYX-HH125K is a powerful all-in-one hybrid inverter designed for large commercial and industrial storage systems, delivering a rated output of 125kW. It integrates PCS, EMS, and BMS into a single compact unit for simplified deployment and efficient management. Supporting up to 10 units in parallel, it offers scalable capacity of up to 1.25MW to meet the demands of large projects. With robust design and advanced control, the system ensures stable performance, seamless integration, and long-term reliability for critical energy applications.

250kW Hybrid Inverter

250kW Three Phase High Voltage IP65

The ORYX-HH250K is a high-capacity all-in-one hybrid inverter built for commercial and industrial energy storage applications, delivering 250kW output in a single compact system. It integrates PCS, EMS, and BMS seamlessly, simplifying installation and management while ensuring reliable operation. 500kW Hybrid Inverter

500kW Three Phase High Voltage IP65
